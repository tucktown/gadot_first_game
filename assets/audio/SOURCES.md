- `music/dark_cavern_loop.ogg` - "Dark Cavern Ambient" by Paul Wortmann,
  continuous-loop version:
  https://opengameart.org/content/dark-cavern-ambient
- `sfx/ui_click.wav`, `sfx/card_play.wav`, and `sfx/block.wav` - selected files

  artisticdude:
  https://opengameart.org/content/swishes-sound-pack
